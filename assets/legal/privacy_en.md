# Privacy policy — Health Tech

## 1. General principles

The application runs locally on the user's Android device.

No personal data is transmitted to the author of the application.

No data is collected remotely.

No data is sold, shared or used for commercial purposes.

---

## 2. Data recorded

The user may record in the application:
- client information;
- follow-up notes;
- appointments;
- information about animals;
- observations related to wellness or support practices.

These data remain stored exclusively on the user's device.

---

## 3. No server connection

The application:
- does not require a user account;
- does not synchronise data over the Internet;
- does not send data to a remote server;
- contains no advertising system;
- contains no tracker or behavioural analytics tool.

---

## 4. Data security

The user remains solely responsible for:
- protecting their device;
- their backups;
- physical access to the phone;
- the confidentiality of the data recorded.

It is recommended to use:
- a password;
- biometric locking;
- device encryption.

---

## 5. Sensitive data

Some information recorded may be considered sensitive under applicable regulations.

The user undertakes to comply with:
- GDPR;
- the legal obligations related to their activity;
- the confidentiality of information concerning their clients.

---

## 6. Android permissions

The application only requests the permissions strictly necessary for its operation.

---

Since the source code of the application is publicly accessible, anyone can verify the actual behaviour of the application.

## 8. Data retention

Data is kept only on the user's device until manually deleted or until the application is uninstalled.
