Health Tech is the professional tool for energetic / Reiki practitioners and wellness facilitators working with humans and animals. It brings your whole practice — clients, animals, sessions, agenda, attachments — into a fully offline Android application, encrypted by default.

🛡️ Built-in security
• Passphrase + biometric unlock (Argon2id 64 MiB, AES-256-GCM).
• SQLCipher database + per-field encryption for health data.
• Configurable auto-lock, FLAG_SECURE enabled (no screenshots).
• No Android auto-backup. No data ever leaves your device.

🐾 Humans and animals
• Client records: identity, contact, address, health history, GDPR consents.
• Animal records: species, breed, identifiers (chip, tattoo, pedigree), vet, behavior.
• Structured sessions: 9-section report (before state, observations, energetic, advice, etc.).
• Agenda: list and monthly view, reminders, status (planned, confirmed, done).

📎 Encrypted attachments
• Photos, prescriptions, consents, scans — AES-256-GCM at rest.
• Automatic detection of malicious images (image bombs).
• Built-in viewer, share via Android system.

📤 GDPR compliance
• Article 15: full ZIP export of a client (records + decrypted attachments).
• Article 17: cascading permanent erasure (client + animals + sessions + attachments).
• Medical disclaimer prompted before every record creation.

🌍 Bilingual FR / EN, optimized for phones and tablets.

🔓 Open source — Apache 2.0 — files-tech.com
